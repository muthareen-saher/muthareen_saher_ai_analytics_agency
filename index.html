Case Studies

Alibaba Sales Analysis Dashboard

Tools Used: Excel | SQL | Microsoft Power BI

Project Overview

This project focuses on building an interactive e-commerce sales intelligence dashboard using an Alibaba retail dataset. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into a structured analytical model that provides visibility into sales performance, product contribution, brand performance, and operational costs.

Business Challenge

The business lacked a centralized reporting system to monitor sales, quantity trends, product performance, shipping costs, and tax impact. Decision-makers were unable to quickly identify revenue drivers, top-performing brands, or sales fluctuations over time.

Solution Approach

The dataset was cleaned and structured using Excel and SQL to create a relational data model. Key calculated metrics were developed, including total sales, total quantity, shipping cost, and tax. The final dashboard was built in Power BI with interactive filters for category, order date, delivery date, product, and delivery status.

Key Features

  • Executive KPI Summary (Sales, Quantity, Products, Categories, Shipping, Tax)
  • Sales Trend Analysis (Revenue vs Quantity over time)
  • Product-Level Revenue & Quantity Analysis
  • Brand Performance Comparison
  • Interactive drill-down filtering capabilities

Key Insights

  • Apple generated the highest revenue contribution.
  • Lenovo led in total quantity sold.
  • Premium products drove revenue spikes more than volume alone.
  • Shipping and tax tracking improved cost visibility.

Business Impact

The dashboard enables real-time performance monitoring, improved pricing decisions, better product strategy planning, and faster executive reporting. It converts raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence.
